Facilities

The sumptuous sitting room is for the exclusive use of guests. Hidden behind the bookcase is a 48" TV and a wide selection of DVDs.

Kitchen

Ranscombe has a large, modern kitchen / living area, with a breakfast bar, two dishwashers, oven, gas hobs, and microwave. There is also an open fireplace with a log-burner.

Utility room

The rear hall, which also has separate external access, leads to the practical elements of the house including a utility room, WC and boiler room.

Accessibility

Due to the style and layout of the property with its spiral staircase, it is not suitable for wheelchair users or those with limited mobility.
