Ranscombe Manor House
Wisteria and Iris Rooms

The Wisteria room can be used as a four-poster double with en-suite for 2 people, or with the inclusion of the Iris room, this can be a spacious family suite for 4 people. In addition, the master room has a sofa bed for 1 or 2 people.

The Iris room is next door to Wisteria.

The bathroom opens out of Wisteria.
